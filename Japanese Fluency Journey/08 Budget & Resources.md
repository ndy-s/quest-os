## Minimum Viable Stack (free only)

If money is tight, this covers the essentials:
- **Anki** (desktop, free): vocab and grammar SRS
- **Tofugu guides** (free): kana learning
- **Genki via library or PDF**: grammar (check your local library first)
- **Comprehensible Japanese** (YouTube, free): listening
- **HelloTalk** (free tier): speaking practice
- **JLPT Sensei** (free): practice tests and grammar reference

You can reach N3 with just these. Bunpro and Wanikani accelerate the process but aren't required.

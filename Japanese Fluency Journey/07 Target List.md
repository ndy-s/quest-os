# Target List — Milestones & Opportunities

## Pitch Narrative

> "I'm learning Japanese from scratch with a structured 12-month plan targeting JLPT N3. I study daily using Anki SRS, Genki textbooks, and Comprehensible Japanese immersion content. By mid-2027 I'll be able to hold basic conversations, read everyday text, and pass JLPT N3."

Use this when introducing yourself on HelloTalk, iTalki, or Japanese learning forums.

---

## Tier 1 — Core Targets (must hit)

| Target | Type | Why | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| JLPT N5 | Exam | Confirms foundation is solid | Offered July and December; register ~3 months ahead |
| JLPT N4 | Exam | Mid-journey checkpoint | Aim for December 2026 sitting |
| JLPT N3 | Exam | Primary goal | Aim for July 2027 sitting; register in March 2027 |
| 3 regular conversation partners | Speaking | Accountability + real output practice | HelloTalk or iTalki; aim for once a week each by Month 6 |
| 30-day Anki streak | Habit | Proof the system is working | Aim for Month 2 |

---

## Tier 2 — Stretch Targets (aim for if on track)

| Target | Type | Why | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 trip to Japan | Immersion | Field test everything | Even a short trip in Month 10–12 is powerful |
| Watch 1 full anime series in Japanese (JP subs) | Immersion | Listening endurance + cultural familiarity | Try Shirokuma Cafe or Yotsuba-level difficulty first |
| Read a full graded reader (Satori Reader story) | Reading | Reading fluency milestone | Target completion in Phase 3 |
| Make 1 Japanese friend online | Social | Real accountability + cultural exchange | HelloTalk or Discord language servers |

---

## Tier 3 — Long-Term (post-N3)

| Target | Type | Why |
|---|---|---|
| JLPT N2 | Exam | Professional level; opens job opportunities in Japan |
| Work or intern in Japan | Career | Meaningful use of the skill |
| Read a manga in Japanese without tools | Reading | True fluency signal |

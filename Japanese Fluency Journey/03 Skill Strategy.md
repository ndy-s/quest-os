# Skill Strategy — Vocabulary & Grammar SRS

## The Core System

Japanese vocabulary and grammar are best learned through spaced repetition: you see an item, recall the answer, and the system schedules the next review based on how well you remembered it. This is not optional — it's the only method that reliably produces long-term retention at the volume required for N3 (~1500 words + ~150 grammar points).

The tracker in `Vocab/Vocab Tracker.md` is your source of truth. Every item you've studied lives there with its review schedule.

---

## One Session: Step by Step

1. Open Anki (or Bunpro for grammar). Do all due reviews first — never skip reviews to add new cards.
2. Check how many reviews are due. If more than 50, do reviews only — no new cards today.
3. If reviews are under 50 and time allows, add new cards: max 5 new vocab + 2 new grammar per day.
4. After Anki, log the session in your daily quest note: how many reviews, how many new, any difficult items.
5. For grammar: after Bunpro, write 1–2 example sentences using the new point. Don't skip this step.

---

## Daily Time Split

| Activity | Time |
|---|---|
| Anki reviews (vocab) | 15–20 min |
| New vocab cards | 5 min |
| Bunpro grammar review | 10 min |
| New grammar (with example sentences) | 10 min |
| **Total core SRS** | ~45 min |

On lighter days (Mon/Thu/Sun), do reviews only. On heavier days (Tue/Fri/Sat), add new cards and spend extra time on grammar.

---

## Review Schedule

Anki manages its own schedule internally. For the tracker (manual logging of key items), use this:

| Review | Interval | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| Review 1 | +1 day | Recall the word/grammar point cold |
| Review 2 | +3 days | Use it in a sentence from memory |
| Review 3 | +7 days | Use it in a sentence about your own life |
| Review 4 | +30 days | Confirm it still feels natural |

## Phase-by-Phase Focus

**Phase 1 (Months 1–3):** Kana only for the first 2 weeks — achieve reading fluency before touching vocab. Then start Core 2000 deck. Grammar: Genki I, chapters 1–6. Target: hiragana + katakana fluent, 300 words, N5 grammar basics.

**Phase 2 (Months 4–8):** Continue Core 2000, move into Core 6000 around month 5. Grammar: Genki I completion + Genki II start, Bunpro N4. Start Comprehensible Japanese (beginner level). Target: 1000 words, N4 grammar coverage.

**Phase 3 (Months 9–12):** Shift focus from new vocab acquisition toward consolidation and active use. Grammar: Bunpro N3. Satori Reader for graded reading. iTalki for speaking. Target: 1500+ words, N3 grammar, can hold a basic 10-minute conversation.

## Deck Recommendations

- **Core 2000** (Anki, free): frequency-based, best starting point
- **JLPT Sensei N5/N4/N3** (Anki, free): JLPT-aligned, good for test alignment in Phase 3
- **Kanji in Context** (Anki, free): kanji with real example sentences
